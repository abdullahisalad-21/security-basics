- Defined cryptography, encryption, cryptosystem, cryptanalysis, frequency analysis, steganography  

### 🔸 Shannon’s Maxim vs Kerckhoffs’s Principle  
- Kerckhoffs: system secure even if everything except key is public  
- Shannon: assume attacker knows the system  

### 🔸 Classical Ciphers  
- Caesar Cipher vs ROT13  
- Frequency analysis weaknesses  

### 🔸 Block Cipher vs Stream Cipher  
- AES vs ChaCha20  
- Padding, speed, use cases  

### 🔸 DES vs AES  
- DES obsolete  
- AES modern standard  

### 🔸 Supplemental Reading: Future of Cryptanalysis  
- KPA, CPA, COA, ACPA, MITM  
- Attack outcomes: instance deduction → total break  
- Quantum computing impact  

### 🔸 RC4 NOMORE Summary  
- RC4 practically breakable  
- HTTPS cookies decryptable in ~75 hours  
- RC4 deprecated  

### 🔸 Symmetric vs Asymmetric Cryptography  
- One key vs key pairs  
- Use cases and weaknesses  

### 🔸 MAC vs HMAC vs CMAC vs CBC‑MAC  
- Integrity + authentication  
- Hash‑based vs AES‑based  

### 🔸 RSA vs DSA vs DH vs ECC vs ECDH vs ECDSA  
- RSA: encrypt + sign  
- DSA: sign only  
- DH: key exchange  
- ECC: modern math  
- ECDH: modern DH  
- ECDSA: modern DSA
